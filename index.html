How does it work?

The inaugral Ryder Cup event took place in 1927 in Massachusetts at the Worcester Country Club. Team USA consists of the top 6 players from the world points ranking while also having 6 captain picks. Team Europe is made up of the top three players from the European Points List while also including the top three players from the World Points List and the remaining players are captain's picks.
